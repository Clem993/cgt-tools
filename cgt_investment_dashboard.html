Red Flags Checklist

Manufacturing Risks

  • Single-source supplier dependency
  • No CDMO capacity secured
  • Batch failure rate >10%
  • Vein-to-vein >4 weeks
  • No comparability data

Clinical Concerns

  • Surrogate endpoints only
  • High dropout rates
  • Unresolved safety signals
  • No dose-response established
  • Small patient population

Commercial Challenges

  • Crowded competitive landscape
  • Pricing >$3M without outcomes data
  • Effective standard of care exists
  • No KOL support
  • Complex administration

Management Red Flags

  • Unrealistic timelines
  • No CMC expertise
  • <18 months runway
  • Frequent strategic pivots
  • No pharma experience
